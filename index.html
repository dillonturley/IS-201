Why Dinosaurs Are Cool

Dinosaurs ruled the Earth for over 160 million years during the Mesozoic Era. They ranged from tiny chicken-sized hunters to massive long-necked giants the size of buildings.

Fun Facts

  • Some dinosaurs had feathers.
  • The fastest dinosaurs could run over 40 mph.
  • Spinosaurus is the only known semi-aquatic dinosaur.
  • Triceratops had horns longer than baseball bats.

The Coolest One

Many paleontologists argue that T. rex remains one of the coolest dinosaurs due to its massive bite force and apex-predator status.
